Prospective students & postdocs

A brief note for people considering joining the lab.

If you are interested in working with us, you are encouraged to send:

  • A short CV
  • A few sentences about your past research experiences
  • What you are excited to explore in microwave/2D quantum materials

Graduate students should apply through the MIT Physics graduate program. Current MIT students (graduate or undergraduate) are very welcome to email Prof. Ji directly to discuss potential projects.
