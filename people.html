Principal Investigator

Zhurun (Judy) Ji
Assistant Professor of Physics, MIT (starting 2025/26)

Judy's research focuses on microwave quantum transport and scanning probes in 2D materials, aiming to understand and program correlated and topological phases. She also cares deeply about building a supportive, thoughtful lab culture.
