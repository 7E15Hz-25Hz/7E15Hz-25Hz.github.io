The Ji Quantum Lab team

We are building a group that is serious about physics and instrumentation, and equally serious about being humane, thoughtful, and collaborative.

Below are the people who are helping to shape Ji Quantum Lab in its early years. As the lab grows, this page will keep track of the faces, projects, and paths our members take.

Principal Investigator

Zhurun (Judy) Ji
Assistant Professor of Physics, MIT (starting 2025/26)

Judy’s research connects microwave techniques with strongly correlated and topological 2D systems. She enjoys working closely with students and postdocs on both the physics story and the experimental details, and cares deeply about making the lab a place where people can grow scientifically and personally.
