Seeing quantum materials in the microwave world.

We build microwave on-tip and on-chip experiments to explore correlated and topological phases in 2D materials, and to learn how to program new quantum states of matter.

We are growing. The lab will formally launch at MIT in 2025–2026. Students and postdocs interested in building new tools and new physics are very welcome.
